# Credentials

After registering for our cloud services, your account will be activated within a few hours, giving you full access to all services.
You can log in using your contact email address and the password you selected during the registration/sign-up form.

If you are unsure about your password, you can reset it by going to the login page and selecting **“Forgot password?”** below the login button.

If you’ve forgotten or want to change your login email address, please contact us at support@envision.nl.
