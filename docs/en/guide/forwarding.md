# Forwarders

Email forwarding automatically redirects incoming emails from one email address to another. This helps manage multiple accounts and ensures you don’t miss important messages.

1. Navigate to the [Emails↗](https://cloud.envision.nl/emails) section.
2. Select the mailbox you want to manage.
3. Scroll down to the Forwarders section.
4. Enter the email address where incoming messages should be forwarded.
